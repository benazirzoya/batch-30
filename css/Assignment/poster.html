Lemon Tea

Health benefits

Lemon tea has many health benefits, including:
Vitamin C: Lemon juice contains vitamin C, which can boost immunity, heal wounds, and prevent scurvy.

Antioxidants

Lemon tea contains antioxidants that can reduce inflammation, fight free radicals, and prevent the flu and common cold.

Heart Health:

Lemon tea contains flavonoids that can lower cholesterol.

Brain health:

Lemon tea contains flavonoids that can lower cholesterol.

Digestive function:

Lemon tea contains dietary fibers and simple sugars that can promote gut health.
